I am Ali Gholami, a researcher at the Technical University of Berlin. I received my PhD with distinction in 2025 in electrical engineering from the Technical University of Berlin with the thesis titled "__Coded Distributed Systems for Content Delivery, Privacy, and Computation__" under supervision of [Prof. Giuseppe Caire](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=g66ErTcAAAAJ&hl=en). Prior to that, I was a master's student at Sharif University of Technology where I worked with [Prof. Mohammad Ali Maddah-Ali](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CFIJZwoAAAAJ&hl=en). I received my bachelor's degree from the University of Tehran. 

My research interests include information theory, coding theory, coded computing for machine learning, and wireless communications.
