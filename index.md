I am Ali Gholami, a researcher at the Technical University of Berlin. I received my PhD in electrical engineering from the __Technical University of Berlin__ with the title __Coded Distributed Systems for Content Delivery, Privacy, and Computation__ under supervision of [Prof. Giuseppe Caire](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=g66ErTcAAAAJ&hl=en). Prior to that, I was a master's student at __Sharif University of Technology__ where I worked with [Prof. Mohammad Ali Maddah-Ali](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CFIJZwoAAAAJ&hl=en). I received my bachelor's degree from the __University of Tehran__. My interests include information theory, coding theory, coded computing for machine learning, and wireless communications.
